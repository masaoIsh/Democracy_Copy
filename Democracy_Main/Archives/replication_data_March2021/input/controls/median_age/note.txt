Data for population density
source link: https://population.un.org/wpp/DataQuery/

Steps:
* Choose "Median age of the total population (years)" as indicator.
* In the next page, select "World" and answer "Yes" to "Do you want to select all the sub-locations as well?"
* Select 2020 as the desired year for which we get data.
* Click on "Export to Excel.""
